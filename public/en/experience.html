Leadership Scope

Oleslav Antamoshkin combines research, education, and project work: he organizes research and engineering teams, and leads technical specifications, architectural decisions, budgets, schedules, and reporting.

In the Siberiana and AirScope projects, he coordinated Siberian Federal University interaction with external organizations, translated research groundwork into working digital products, and prepared pilot scenarios for customers.
